Science Fair style project

With multiple weeks to work with children can take their time on all the steps in the scientific process.
  • Ask a question.
  • Create a hypothesis.
  • Conduct & document experiment.
  • Did new questions / hypothesis arise from the previous steps?
  • Document and share with friends & family!
  • Some ideas to get the wheels turning...
  1. Rates that different objects freeze / melt /etc.
  2. How volume changes
  3. Which materials dissolve, float, evaporate, etc.
  4. Trends/habits related to left/right handedness
