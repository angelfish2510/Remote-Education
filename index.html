General Concept / Schedule

I hope to create opportunity for learning during this extended break and make sure to keep it fun. Ideally we will all stay more sane with a routine and giving the kids choices should keep up their engagement.

Bingo Cards

  • Bingo cards created for each grade for the following subjects:
    • Math
    • Science
    • Writing
    • Reading
  • When 5 in a row (any direction) are marked off they get to select a small prize (prize includes fun activities!)

Grab Bag

    • Make slime

    • Puzzles
