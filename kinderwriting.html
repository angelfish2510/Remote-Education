Kindergarten Writing

Writing every day for practice with organizing thoughts, overcoming spelling blocks, understanding what we can do with words.

Independent Writing Projects

Can include an illustration, can be 1-3 sentences.

Descriptive Writing

  • "My Favorite Birthday"
  • "My Favorite Day of School"
  • "My Favorite First"
  • and more!

Correspondence

  • Letters or emails to friends
  • Letters or emails to family

Creative Writing

  • Wacky Wednesday themes
  • If ____ (animal) could talk it would say _____
  • Things I want to know more about

Cause and Effect

  • I feel ____ because/when _____
  • When I _____ this happens

Other Writing

  • short story
  • lists (pros/cons, groceries, tasks, etc.)
